Instructions To Follow

  • ● Arrive on time to avoid delays.
  • ●  Bring necessary documents and appointment slip(digital/print).
  • ● Make a list of questions or concerns beforehand.
  • ● Describe symptoms clearly and in detail.
  • ● Ask for clarification if you don't understand something.
  • ● Follow the doctor's instructions for medication and treatment.
  • ● Discuss a follow-up plan before leaving.
  • ● Take notes or bring a trusted companion for support.
  • ● Settle billing and schedule future appointments if needed.
  • ● Be an active participant in your healthcare journey.
